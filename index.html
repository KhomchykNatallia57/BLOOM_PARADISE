stages of work

Acceptance of application and review of project

We conduct a small survey on tasks and wishes by phone, email, or WhatsApp. You send us a photo/video or a design project of a space that needs to be landscaped. Upon approval, it is possible to visit the facility for a more correct selection of offers.

01

Offer formation

Based on the information we have collected, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with ready-made versions from our catalog or prepare a KP with visualizations for your project, and send them to you.

02

Approval of the estimate and signing of the contract

We make a detailed estimate of the landscaping project and send it to you. After successful approval, we send the contract and the invoice for payment.

03

Project implementation

After signing the contract and paying for it, we begin to prepare your order: we select and purchase healthy and beautiful plants, and we launch the production of planters. Next, we coordinate the day and carry out delivery and installation at the facility. We bring the transplanted ready-made compositions and put them in their places.
